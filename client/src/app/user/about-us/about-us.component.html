Narrative Section

Here is where you can go crazy writing. You can talk about a wide variety of concepts here, even though it was named "narrative". To give you some examples, you can elaborate the story behind your game. Flesh out what the intro might be and how the character comes into your adventure.

No story line? No problem! You could also elaborate on how you came up with the game, talk a bit about yourself or even make a character sheet for your protagonist. You make the rules!

*Spoiler alert*

Remember to be careful on how detailed you write out your story. Some people prefer to write every detail to make sure the reader knows what they're getting into. Others prefer a brief synopsis that has just enough information to spark curiosity and hopefully have the reader play the game. See what works for you.


Art Section

Here you can include sprite sheets, screen shots of a 3D model, concept art, scenery, brainstorming doodles and more! You can elaborate on why you went with the art style you picked and if it has any meaning to the game itself. Of course, there usually is a reason so go crazy! You don't have to be a full fledged professional artist to say "I picked it because I liked it!". Feel free to also elaborate on the programs you used, any difficulties you encountered and even trashed concepts! It's always fun to go behind the scenes.
